    Who are Chamet SubAgents ?

  • All other agents that you invite to join Chamet as an agency using your invitation link are your Chamet SubAgents. since each new agent needs to be invited by an existing agent. Thus, you act as his Upper Agent (or mentor) and he acts as your sub-agent when you invite a new Chamet Agent.
  • Like you, your subAgent receives payment directly from Chamet, has his own dashboard, and independently oversees his team.
  • What are the advantages for you?
  • Recall that the total revenue your hosts and sub-agents in your agency generate determines your commission ratio as an agent.
  • TOTAL REVENUE OF AGENCY = ( REVENUE OF HOSTS + REVENUE OF SUB-AGENTS )
  • Your commission ratio % will be determined each week depending on the total revenue of your agency.
  • Example :
  • Assume you have four subagents.
  • With a total income of $1,500, the first sub-agent has a 15% ratio.
  • With a 500$ income, the second person's ratio is 10%.
  • Since the third person makes $500, his ratio is 10%.
  • With an income of $5,000, the Forth individual's ratio is 20%.
  • Furthermore, suppose you only have sub-agents instead of hosts. The computation of your commission is as follows:

  • Your ratio is 20% since the total revenue for you and your subagents is 1.500+500+500+5.000=7.500$. Then, each sub-agent will pay you a commission that looks like this:
  • First sub-agent: 15% - 20% = 5% x 1500 = 75 $,
  • The Second : 20% - 10% = 10% x 500=50$.
  • The Third : 20% - 10% = 10% x 500=50$.
  • The Fourth : 20% - 20% = 0$, no commission from him.

  • Thus, in total, you will have 75+50+50+0= 175$ commission only from your 4 sub-agents.
  • Since we previously discussed, your current commission ratio is 20% if you also have direct hosts who made $1500. Consequently, you will receive a commission of $1500 x 20% = $300 from your host.

  • Your revenue will grow by increasing the number of sub-agents, which is Chamet most significant feature.
